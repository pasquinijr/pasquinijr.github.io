Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 28, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Verses By Heart (“the App”) handles information when you use it.

Summary

  • The App does not collect or store your personal data.
  • Your preferences and saved verses are stored on your device and iCloud.
  • The App fetches verse content from a remote service, but does not send your personal data to it.

Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information, analytics, advertising identifiers, or usage data.

App Store Privacy Disclosure

  • Data collected: none.
  • Data linked to you: none.
  • Data used to track you: none.
  • Data shared with third parties: none.

The only network activity is fetching verse content; requests do not include personal information, saved verses, or preferences.

Information Stored on Your Device

The App stores your preferences and any saved verses locally on your device. If you have iCloud enabled for the App, this data may also be stored in your iCloud account so it can sync across your devices.

iCloud data is associated with your Apple ID and is not accessible to us.

Remote Services (Verse Content)

The App connects to a backend service to fetch verse content. This request does not include personal information or your saved data.

Children’s Privacy

The App is intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children or any other users.

Account Creation

The App does not currently offer account creation or sign-in. If this changes in the future, we will update this Privacy Policy before any account features are enabled.

Changes to This Policy

If we change this policy, we will update the “Last updated” date at the top of this page.
